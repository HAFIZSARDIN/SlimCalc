WHAT IS BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index)

is a simple numerical measure of a person's weight relative to their height. It is commonly used to assess whether an individual has a healthy body weight

BMI CALCULATE FORMULA:

BMI = Weight (kg) Height (m) 2 BMI= Height (m) 2 Weight (kg) ​
Alternatively, for weight in pounds and height in inches, the formula is: BMI = Weight (lb) × 703 Height (in) 2 BMI= Height (in) 2 Weight (lb)×703 ​

BMI Categories:

Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 24.9
Overweight: 25 ≤ BMI < 29.9
Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
