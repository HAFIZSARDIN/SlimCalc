Advantage of Taking Care of BMI

Prevent Heart Disease

Maintaining a balanced BMI helps reduce the risk of heart disease and cardiovascular problems such as high blood pressure and heart attacks.

Maintaining a healthy BMI not only reduces the risk of heart disease, but also helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels, which are important for overall heart health.

Reduces the Risk of Diabetes

UKeeping your BMI in a healthy range helps reduce the risk of type 2 diabetes, which is often associated with obesity and being overweight.

A balanced BMI helps control blood sugar levels, making the body more responsive to insulin, thus reducing the risk of insulin resistance.

Improves Respiratory Health

A high BMI can cause fat to accumulate around the chest and abdomen, which puts pressure on the lungs and reduces breathing capacity

Excessive body tightness can interfere with breathing and increase the risk of sleep apnea. Maintaining a healthy BMI improves breathing and reduces sleep disturbances.

Increases Energy and Stamina

With a healthy BMI, the body is more efficient in metabolism and energy use, which means you will feel more energetic for daily activities.

Maintaining a healthy BMI also helps the cardiovascular system function better
